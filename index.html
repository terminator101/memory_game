Click on cards to flip them over. If they match, you will get a point, if not, try again or pass the mouse to the next person (if playing with multiple people). The top part of the screen displays the player whose turn it is. The bottom part shows the scores for the individual players. For now the only way to play against other people is by passing the mouse, but I do plan to add computer players in the future and eventually make the game playable online against other people. Enjoy!
